What is FAANG?

FAANG is an acronym for the five most popular and best-performing tech stocks in the market, namely Facebook, Apple, Amazon, Netflix, and Google (now Alphabet). These five tech giants have been leading the technology sector for years and have shown no signs of slowing down. They are known for their innovative products and services, strong financial performance, and dominant market positions.
